World is beautiful

Technology and efficiency are features of the 21st century. Thanks to them, our life becomes comfortable, its duration and quality increase.

New and better inventions appear every day. Much thanks to programming. Nowadays, everyone should learn programming, whether they become a developer or not.

If you think about how amazing it is. When we write code, we tell the dumbest thing in the world what to do. Thus, affecting the real world. These can be very simple and routine tasks, or those that are beyond the power of a person or that take years to complete.

Hi there. My name is Alexander. I want to make this world better. But, I can't do it alone.

Almost no one can do something very significant alone. The time of personalities is over. It's time for groups, companies, communities and so on.

Well, let's think about what kind of world we live in. On the one hand, progress in almost all spheres of life. Quality of life, medicine, food, working conditions, leisure ... Everything is getting better.

On the other hand, there are still many negative things that have no place in the civilized world. Poverty, deadly diseases, famine, war ...

That is, we have the most perfect brain and can not get rid of everything that prevents us from living peacefully? It just can't be. This is clearly not the case.

The universe is so incredible, even hard to imagine. Here's what to work on. But we can't even agree with each other. We continue to argue, to fight. Why? Why can't we build housing for all people and feed them? We need to understand that. Together.

I think technology is our only way out. With the help of technology, we can not only survive but also get better.

Yes, many professions will disappear, but many new ones will appear.

More and more things will become automated. We will be able to meet all basic needs.

For some, it will mean more free time. Which can be spent with family and friends, have fun, or be alone with your thoughts. For some, it will be an opportunity to do something new or become better in their profession. Start a hobby. Move progress forward.

Someone will look at the stars and in the future someone will reach them.

All this is possible. And it depends on each of us. From what we do every moment.
